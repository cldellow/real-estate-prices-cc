The City of Langley is centered at the junction of Highways 10 and 200st in the lower Fraser Valley area with easy highway access to Vancouver and Surrey. Langley is strategically located between the large business centers of Surrey and Abbotsford, and is one of the fastest growing suburban communities in BC.
